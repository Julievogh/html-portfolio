The best movies according to Julie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Vanilla Sky

    This is my favourite movie of all time! It is surrealistic, beautiful to behold, weird, romantic and malancolic.
    You will either love it or hate it. According to me, it is art as good as a Monet painting!

  2. Bohemian Rapsody

    Those who did not watch this movie in the cinema missed out on a beautiful experience!
    I never heard clapping after a movie - but after this most people clapped and then sat quietly thinking about
    the experience they had just witnessed. Wonderful film!

  3. The Whale

    This is a more current film, that I really enjoyed. It deals with the most ugly parts of humankind, but also the most pure.
    I rolled out of the cinema gasping for air
